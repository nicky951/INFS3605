Preventative Actions you can take

If you want to be safe working from home amidst the current COVID and WFH environment, you NEED to adhere to the following protocols:

  • Do not open any suspicious looking email nor click on dodgy website links.
  • Do not download and execute any attached .exe files unless they have been subject to an antivirus check and are approved from your company IT department.
  • Do not store usernames and passwords on our personal devices, such as your phone or computer. Make sure to use a Password Manager!
  • Adhere to network restrictions and avoid accessing social media and the Google Suite.
  • Back up information and data repositories weekly and store them on company-secure offline servers biweekly.
  • Do not store private and confidential information on your personal devices as they may fall into malicious hands if stolen or misplaced.
  • Make use of company VPNs when working away from the office to ensure your connection to the company network is secure and cannot be snooped.
  • Do not disable any technical controls provided to you by the IT department in your organisation as they are designed to protect you and this could violate their policies.
  • Scan for antivirus updates regularly as they are constantly updated with new forms of malware and viruses.
